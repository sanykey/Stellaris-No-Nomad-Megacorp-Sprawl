# No Nomad Megacorp Sprawl

**English** | [Русский](README.ru.md)

This mod removes the **+50% Empire Size from Colonies** penalty applied by the [**Corporate**](https://stellaris.paradoxwikis.com/Government#Corporate) authority when it is used by a nomadic empire.

In the base game, a Megacorporation receives **+50% Empire Size from Colonies**. On its own, this penalty is manageable. Nomads also receive **+100% Empire Size from Colonies**, which already places a significant limit on their expansion. When combined, however, these penalties stack, causing a nomadic Megacorporation's Empire Size to grow excessively quickly.

In practice, this can make the early game extremely difficult. For example, by 2245, just four colony ships can contribute around **+200 Empire Size**. This makes it much harder to reach the ascension that is supposed to help mitigate these penalties. For **Evolutionary Predators**, the penalty may even remain after ascension.

This mod makes the Megacorporation penalty more reasonable: the **+50% penalty from the Corporate authority applies only to settled empires**, not to nomads, who already have a substantial penalty of their own.

The same change also applies to [**BioPredation GLC**](https://stellaris.paradoxwikis.com/Government#BioPredation_GLC).
